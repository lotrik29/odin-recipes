Light and Easy Greek Potato Salad

Description

This potato salad is both light and easy to make. The recipe can easily be multiplied to make more for huge family gatherings or get togethers!

Ingredients

  • 12 red potatoes
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon coarse salt, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon dried rosemary, crumbled
  • 1 pinch ground red pepper

Steps

  1. Slice potatoes and place into a large salad bowl
  2. toss potatoes with green onion
  3. Whisk olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, oregano, sugar, rosemary, and red pepper in a bowl
  4. Pour the dressing over the potatoes and toss
  5. Serve immediately or chill until serving time.
